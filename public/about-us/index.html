    2017 Texas Custom Builder of the Year

    THE ON POINT MISSION

    We are committed to you: the client

    On Point Custom Homes is a locally and nationally recognized Houston custom home builder and remodeler with a full-service Design + Build team. Our level of service speaks for itself in our expert design experience and innovative building approach, encouraging client involvement with our experienced staff from conception through completion. Our constant drive to stay ahead of the building curve provides an unparalleled building and remodeling experience which set us apart in today’s market.

    The On Point Custom Homes team is comprised of architects, designers and seasoned construction professionals who work together to provide distinctly unique and beautiful floor plans to suit a wide variety of buyers. A custom home or renovation is the ultimate reflection of the owner, and getting to know our clients is the first step – setting the foundation for a constructive relationship that will lead to a satisfied client and a beautiful home. Our design + builds take into account the needs of each individual client, whether it be a desire for a modern multi-story home with clean lines and a spacious, multi-functioning living space or a more traditionally styled high-end product with cutting-edge technology set within a richly finished, well-defined layout.

    Design + Build Process

    Award Winning Homes

    We’re proud to be the recipients of numerous design and construction awards, beginning with the GHBA’s Best Custom Home Elevation in 2009. Since then, we’ve gratefully received numerous accolades from our peers in the building industry and multiple publications.

    Experience you can count on

    Our design, our process, and the building systems we embrace may be cutting edge, but our seasoned team has withstood the test of time. Our Certified Graduate Builders have been working hands-on for decades, and have successfully built countless homes to the same, exacting standards we’ll apply to yours.

    Innovation

    Cutting edge in both form and function, On Point is continually in pursuit of better building systems and design trends. Education–for ourselves and our clients–is paramount in our process.

    Sustainability

    Our staff is comprised of Certified Green Builders. The homes we design + build are thoughtfully designed, cleanly built, energy-efficient, and environmentally conscious.

    Value

    Quality is our top concern. But offering a strong value is also critical to our mission. Our full-service approach to designing and building your custom home means a comprehensive fee that helps keep you budget under control from start to finish. And we’re members of CB-USA, which gives us immediate access to pricing and allows us to turn around timely and accurate proposals.

    Always on the move

    We work hard to keep things interesting. Check out the latest news, keep up with design trends, and see what our people are up to.
